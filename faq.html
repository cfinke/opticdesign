Q: Why is it called Optic Design?
"Optic Design is named accordingly because, as you may have noticed, Web design is a visual medium. You can't taste it, smell it, or touch it. Occasionally, you might hear a sound byte, but that's a whole separate world. So 'Optic,' meaning "pertaining to the eye or sight" describes the medium perfectly."

Q: Do I have to choose a design package?
"No. You can, but you don't have to. You can pay by the individual element, or you can choose a package and then pay for each extra element that you want added."

Q: How do your prices compare to the rest of the market?
"Optic Design is priced on the low end of the design scene. With the amount of talent at work, we depend on volume of customers to make up for the extra money we don't charge."

Q: Do you offer domain hosting?
"No. We are first and foremost a design firm. We use the service of Affordable Host for hosting and registration."

Q: What is a 'favorites' icon, or 'favicon'?
"A favicon is the little picture you see next to the name of the site when you add it to your favorites in Internet Explorer 5.0 and above."
