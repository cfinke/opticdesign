Chris Finke, Designer

Chris has several years experience in Web design. He heads up the design team, keeping things together. His areas of specialty are HTML markup, Flash, computer graphics, and layout.

Examples of Chris's work are seen in the graphics for this site, as well as the layouts of RealJerry.com and InstaShock.com. His preferred programs include Notepad, Paint Shop Pro, and Macromedia's Flash 4.
